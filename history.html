Our History

Abhishek Methodist Church has been a beacon of faith, hope, and love in our community. Explore our journey through the years.

Our Beginnings

Grace Community Church began in 1975 when a small group of families felt called to establish a new church focused on biblical teaching and community service. Meeting initially in a local school gymnasium, these founding members laid the groundwork for what would become a thriving spiritual home for generations to come.

Through faith, perseverance, and God's guidance, our church has grown from those humble beginnings to become a vibrant congregation that continues to impact lives throughout our community and beyond.

Our Journey Through the Years

1975

Church Founded

Abhishek Methodist Church was established with just 25 members meeting in a local school gymnasium.

1980

First Building

Our first church building was constructed, providing a permanent home for our growing congregation.

1995

Community Outreach Begins

We launched our first major community outreach programs, including a food pantry and after-school programs.

2005

Building Expansion

A major expansion added our current sanctuary, education wing, and fellowship hall to accommodate growth.

2015

40th Anniversary

We celebrated 40 years of ministry with special events and the establishment of our mission fund.

2020

Online Ministry Launch

In response to global challenges, we expanded our digital presence to reach people beyond our walls.

Present

Continuing the Journey

Today, we continue to grow and adapt while staying true to our founding mission and values.

Our Legacy Continues

As we look to the future, we remain committed to building on the strong foundation laid by those who came before us. We honor our past while embracing new opportunities to share God's love with our changing world.
