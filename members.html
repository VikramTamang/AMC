Becoming a Member

We welcome anyone interested in becoming part of our church family. Here's how you can take the next steps toward membership.

1

Attend a Welcome Class

Our monthly welcome classes provide an overview of our church's history, beliefs, values, and opportunities for involvement. It's a great way to learn more and ask questions.

Next Class: First Sunday of each month after the 11:00 AM service

2

Meet with a Pastor

Schedule a conversation with one of our pastors to share your faith journey, ask questions, and discuss how you might connect with our church community.

3

Membership Commitment

After completing the welcome class and meeting with a pastor, you'll be invited to make a membership commitment, which includes embracing our statement of faith and values.

4

Welcome Ceremony

New members are welcomed during a Sunday service, where the congregation has the opportunity to meet you and commit to supporting you in your faith journey.

Ready to take the next step? Contact us to express your interest in membership.
