About Our Church

Grace Community Church has been serving our community since 1975, providing a place of worship, fellowship, and spiritual growth.

Our Mission

At Grace Community Church, our mission is to love God, love people, and make disciples. We strive to create an environment where everyone can experience God's love and grow in their faith.

Our Vision

We envision a community transformed by the love of Christ, where people from all walks of life come together to worship, learn, and serve. We aim to be a beacon of hope and a catalyst for positive change in our community and beyond.

Our Values

  • Biblical teaching and spiritual growth
  • Inclusive and welcoming community
  • Compassionate service to others
  • Authentic worship and prayer
  • Intergenerational ministry and mentorship
